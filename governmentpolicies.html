Government Policies for Farmers

PM-Kisan Scheme

The PM-Kisan scheme provides income support to all landholding farmer families in India. Eligible farmers receive direct income support to help cover agricultural expenses.

For more details, visit the official PM-Kisan Portal.

Crop Insurance (PMFBY)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) provides insurance to farmers to protect them from financial losses due to crop failure caused by natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

To know more, visit the official PMFBY Portal.

Soil Health Card Scheme

The Soil Health Card scheme offers farmers soil testing and recommendations on appropriate dosages of nutrients to increase soil productivity.

Get details on the official Soil Health Portal.

Market Access Initiatives

Initiatives such as eNAM (National Agriculture Market) provide farmers with access to nationwide markets, improving transparency and offering better pricing options.

For more information, visit the eNAM Portal.
